The artist behind the brush

Self-taught, soul-driven.

I'm Ruchika — founder of Meraki Creation. I never went to art school; I taught myself watercolor and acrylic by chasing colors that made me feel something. What started as small sketches became a studio full of moonlit skies, character art, and portraits so real they feel alive.

Today I create custom-made artwork for people all over — birthday gifts, pet portraits, wedding keepsakes, and statement pieces for cozy walls. If you can picture it, I can paint it.
